Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 17, 2026

At Logo Maker, we prioritize the privacy and security of our customers. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better shopping experience, including:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, and phone number when you register or make a purchase.
  • Delivery Data: Physical address for shipping purposes.
  • Authentication Data: We use Google Identity Services for secure social login and Google Authenticator for Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for:

  • Processing and delivering your orders.
  • Providing secure access to your account.
  • Sending order confirmations and support messages via FormSubmit.co.
  • Enhancing website security and preventing fraud.

3. Data Security & Third Parties

We do not sell your data. We use industry-standard services to keep your information safe:

  • Paystack: All payments are processed securely. Your card details are never stored on our servers.
  • Google: Used for secure authentication via OAuth 2.0.
  • FormSubmit.co: Used to protect your email and handle contact form submissions reliably.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal data at any time through your account settings or by contacting our support team.
